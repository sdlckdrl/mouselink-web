Terms of Use

These terms describe the basic conditions for using the OneMouse app and website.

01

Service summary

OneMouse connects a Windows PC, Android device, and paired Windows PCs over a local network for input control, file transfer, and clipboard features.

02

Local network requirements

The app generally requires the PC, Android device, and paired PCs to be on the same local network. Guest Wi-Fi, AP isolation, company security policy, firewalls, and antivirus software may prevent connection.

03

Free / Pro

Free mode keeps the basic connection flow. Pro unlocks repeated-use limits such as ads, transfer limits, saved PCs, and advanced input. Pro purchases are handled by Google Play Billing in the Android app.

04

Ads and billing

Free mode ads are displayed through Google Mobile Ads SDK. Google Play Billing is used for Pro purchases and purchase restoration.

05

Support scope

OneMouse is provided for Windows PC, Android, and paired PC connections on the same Wi-Fi or local network. USB/ADB connection is not included in the user-facing support scope.

06

Limitations

OneMouse depends on network state, operating system permissions, security software, and device manufacturer behavior. Continuous connection and transfer cannot be guaranteed in every environment.

Terms of Use

These terms describe the basic conditions for using the OneMouse app and website.

1. Service summary

OneMouse connects a Windows PC and Android device over a local network for input control, file transfer, and clipboard features.

2. Local network requirements

The app generally requires the PC and Android device to be on the same local network. Guest Wi-Fi, AP isolation, company security policy, firewalls, and antivirus software may prevent connection.

3. Free / Pro

Free mode keeps the basic connection flow. Pro unlocks repeated-use limits such as ads, transfer limits, saved PCs, and advanced input. Pro purchases are handled by Google Play Billing in the Android app.

4. Ads and billing

Free mode ads are displayed through Google Mobile Ads SDK. Google Play Billing is used for Pro purchases and purchase restoration.

5. Experimental paths

USB/ADB connection paths are emergency or experimental features. They may require developer options, ADB approval, or specific PC setup and are not guaranteed as the primary user path.

6. Limitations

OneMouse depends on network state, operating system permissions, security software, and device manufacturer behavior. Continuous connection and transfer cannot be guaranteed in every environment.

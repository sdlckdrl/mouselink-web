Open Source Licenses

OneMouse uses open source libraries and third-party SDKs for the PC app, Android app, QR scanning, billing, and ads.

Android

AndroidX and Kotlin libraries are used for Android app implementation. ZXing-based components are used for QR scanning.

Google SDKs

Google Play Billing Library is used for OneMouse Pro purchases and purchase restoration. Google Mobile Ads SDK is used for Free mode banner ads.

PC app

The Windows app uses Electron and Python components for the desktop UI, local discovery, input capture, and transfer features.
